Featured Projects

Hello! I am a Bolivian graphic designer with strong focus on typography.
The following featured projects highlight some of my style of work.

RUMPUS Typeface

The latest type family I've designed, RUMPUS is inspired by Rock festivals and concerts. It is intended to add personality to the content in these kind of events without risking too much on legibility.

Graphic Design for UCB - La Paz

Here are various graphic designs created for Universidad Católica Boliviana "San Pablo," one of Bolivia's leading universities, where I am currently a member of the design department team.

20D - Logo design for the 20th Anniversary of the Graphic Design Carreer in UCB

The objective behind this logo was to come up with a bold solution that could be sent to the carrer's designer friends and let them freely design a piece.
