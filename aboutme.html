About Me

Who am I?

I’m a graphic designer with a passion for crafting meaningful, timeless designs that connect with audiences. My focus is on typography and branding, where I believe type is not just a tool but a form of expression that is capable of providing a voice. For me, design is all about finding that sweet spot between creativity and functionality, delivering solutions that are not only visually striking but also strategic. My work is driven by the principles of clarity and simplicity.
I am currently located in La Paz-Bolivia, but I am willing to relocate.
